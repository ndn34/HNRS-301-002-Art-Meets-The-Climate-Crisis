About Us

This site was created by Nicholas Nguyen and Kevin Fang as a project for our class Art Meets the Climate Crisis. We are Computer Science Majors at Drexel Univsersity. We decided to create a website as we felt this was the way we could show our understanding the best as compared to a presentation or something similar. We selected areas that can show people the things that they do often in their own lives. We split the work for the sight in half. Nicholas created the Electricity, Waste, Conclusion, and this About Us page. Kevin worked on the Home Page, Technology, and Transportation pages.
