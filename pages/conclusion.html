Conclusion

There are many things we do everyday that contribute to climate change. Some of those things are more impactful than others. We hope this site shed light on some of the everyday habits that are worth reconsidering. How can we expect change to happen if we are not willing to change ourselves? We must be cognizant of our behaviors and make change within ourselves when we can. However, we fear that some will turn a blind eye to industrial contributions that we the lay people are not aware of. We spoke of how data centers are highly polluting and take immense amounts of energy. These types of things are common in many industries. Meaningful change in the climate will only come as a result of both. Companies will not budge until people demand it, and people will not demand it until they understand what is at stake. The Anthropocene may be the human made epoch. However, we have the ability to change it and instead of the great human failure it can be the great human recovery.
