What is the Anthropocene?

The “Anthropocene” is a proposed term for a new geological epoch where human activity has become a dominant force shaping Earth’s climate, ecosystems, and even the physical layers of the planet. In other words, our everyday decisions—energy use, consumption, transportation, and waste—leave lasting marks that can be detected on a global scale.

This site uses interactive estimates to make those impacts easier to feel and compare—turning abstract climate data into something personal and visible.
