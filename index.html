What is the Anthropocene?

The “Anthropocene” is a proposed term for a new geological epoch in which human activity has become a dominant force shaping Earth’s climate, ecosystems, and even the physical layers of the planet. In simple terms, everyday human actions such as energy use, consumption, transportation, and waste production leave measurable marks on the planet that can be detected on a global scale.

This site uses interactive estimates to help make those impacts easier to understand and compare. The goal is to turn abstract climate data into something more personal and visible by connecting environmental change to ordinary daily habits.
